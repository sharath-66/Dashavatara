Parashurama Avatar

The Story of PARASHURAMA Avatar


In Parashurama Avatar, Lord Vishnu incarnates himself as a brahmana (priest) in this world.
He was brought in this world to avenge all kshatriyas who had become arrogant and were suppressing the brahmans in the world.
He was born to Jamadagni and Renuka, and belonged to the Brighu clan.
Parashurama was always carrying an axe presented to him by Lord Shiva of whom he was an ardent devotee.
Kartavirya Arjuna(also called Sahastra Arjuna) a powerful king and his army, once went to Jamadagni's home, was fed with delicious food with offerings from Jamadagni's divine cow Kamadhenu
The King demanded the cow for the betterment of his subject.
Jamadagni refused because he needed the cow for his religious ceremonies.
King Kartavirya Arjuna sent his soldiers to take the cow.
As the conflict developed Kartavirya lost his cool and chopped off the head of Jamadagni.
After hearing the context from his mother(Renuka Devi also considered the incarnation of Shakthi i.e., Durga) Parashurama was enraged at this and went and avenged the death of his father killing Kartavirya and also all kshatriyas (Kings who were looting common men) in 21 battles.

Analysis:
Not just Asuras(evil soul) the Lord also protects us from other humans who loot us.
The lord is present in one and everybody's soul. That's what is said by Shree Adi Shankaracharya in his Advaita Vedanta
Advaita refers to its idea that the soul(Atma) is the same as the highest metaphysical reality(Brahma).
Advaita literally mean "not-two".
Here it can be observed that Parashurama isn't actually Vishnu but a piece of his eternal soul as Parashurama is even mentioned in Ramayana and is present in anger when Rama broke ShivaDhanush(the bow of Shiva), and will also continue to be till Kalki Avatar where he'll become Kalki's guru and teaches him use of all the weaponary.
