Vamana Avatar

The Story of VAMANA Avatar


In Vamana Avatar, Lord Vishnu incarnates himself as a dwarf priest in this world.
Bali, the grandson of Prahlada was a very valorous and mighty asura..
By his penance and might, he conquered the whole world..
Indra and other gods fearing that he and asuras would conquer all the three worlds, went to Lord Vishnu for help..
Lord Vishnu was then born as a dwarf Vamana in the household of a brahmana (priest)..
He went to Bali on growing up and asked for alms..
Bali was delighted to offer him anything he requested even though his priest warned him that it was Lord Vishnu..
Vamana then requested for the amount of land that could come under his three feet..
Bali gracefully agreed. Lord Vishnu then grew in size and covered the earth and heaven in two stride..
And due to lack of space, he put his third leg on Bali himself and crushed Bali to the nether or the Patala loka(underground world), thus helping the Gods out..

Analysis:
The incarnation of Vamana can define that if you are a follower of Dharma and stands by the Dharma not even the lord can kill you.
Here Bali, the grandson of Prahlada, though an asura(evil soul) was the follower of Dharma
So it is obvious that among all the incarnation of lord (except for Matsya Avatar, Kurma Avatar and Buddha Avatar where establishment of Human and Sura's was most important) was taken to KILL The EVIL
but in this Avatar he couldn't kill the asura king BALI because he was on the path of Dharma.
This the Avatar which supports one of if not the most famous shlok --------->
"Dharm~Eva Hota Hanti, Dharmo Rakshati Rakshitaha!
Tasma-Dharmo Na Hantvyo, Ma No Dharmo Hato-Vadhit"
which says
"Dharma destroys the destroyer; Dharma protects the protector.
Therefore, Dharma doesnot destroy, nor Dharma can be destroyed."
Scientific Analysis of the above said shlok
 Eventhough I might seem like I'm going a bit off course but this explanation is very much necessary in-order for us to understand any incarnation of the Lord.
It's explanation when I compare it with Gravitational force is as follows:

If Gravitational force is destroyed, everything on earth will fall apart;Gravitational force keeps everything protected on earth.
Neither Gravitational force can be destroyed, nor does Gravitational force destroy.

 So it can be seen that Rishi's and Muni's of our ancient time used to respect science as the above example stands true as per law of conservation of energy. And that even the Lord is bound by the physics law.
